How to Use the PayPal Calculator

1.Enter the Amount

In the "Enter Amount" input field, type the amount of money you want to calculate the PayPal fee for.

2.Select Your Country

From the "Select Country" dropdown menu, choose the country you are in. This will populate the fee structures specific to that country.

3.Choose the Fee Type

After selecting a country, a new dropdown will appear allowing you to select the fee type. This will show different fee structures available for the selected country, including percentage and fixed fee details.

4.Calculate Fees:

Click the "Calculate" button to determine the PayPal fees. The system will use the selected fee type and country information to calculate the fee, how much you will receive, and how much you should ask for.Results will be displayed in a card format.
