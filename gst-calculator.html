How to Use the GST Calculator

1.Enter the Amount

Input the amount you want to calculate GST for in the "Amount" field.

2.Select GST Percentage:

Choose the appropriate GST rate from the "GST %" dropdown menu (e.g., 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%).

3.Choose Tax Type:

Select "Inclusive" if GST is already included in the amount, or "Exclusive" if GST needs to be added.

4.View Results:

Click "Submit" to calculate. The Actual Amount, GST Amount, and Total Amount will be displayed.
